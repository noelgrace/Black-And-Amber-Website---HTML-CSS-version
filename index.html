Black & Amber is a website dedicated to Kilkenny hurling greats during the Brian Cody era of 1999 to present date. The current Kilkenny Team team which includes some of the greatest Kilkenny hurlers of all time such as Richie Hogan, Michael Fennelly and TJ Reid is managed by the legendary Brian Cody. This team has been described by many leading GAA pundits on The Sunday Game such as Ger Loughnane, Liam Sheedy, Cyrill Farrell and Donal Óg Cusack as the greatest team of all time. Kilkenny have won an unprecedented 36 All-Ireland Senior Titles including an historic four in a row 2006-2009 and they lead the roll of honour.

2015 Season

Kilkenny celebrated winning their 36th All-Ireland title in 2015 another monumental achievement for what has been described as the Greatest Team of all Time.

All Stars










Seven Kilkenny hurlers won a place on the 2015 All Stars Team. Well done to Paul Murphy, Joey Holden, Cillian Buckley, Michael Fennelly, Richie Hogan, T J Reid and Ger Aylward. It was first time awards for Joey and Ger.

The GAA, GPA and Opel presented 15 hurlers for the 2015 season with All-Star hurling awards. Recent All-Ireland champions Kilkenny led the haul with a magnificent 7 awards.All-Ireland finalists Galway fared very well obtaining 4 awards. Waterford’s strong 2015 showing saw them earn league honours before reaching a Munster final and All-Ireland semi-final. This form was awarded in the Deise claiming 3 All-Star awards including the Young Hurler of the Year which went to Tadhg De Burca. Munster champions Tipperary claimed just one award for 2015 despite their form in winning the Munster Championship.

The award ceremony took place on Friday November 6 2015 in the National Convention Centre in Dublin.
